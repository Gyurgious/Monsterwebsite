モンスター

Monster is a psychological thriller anime series that premiered in 2004. Created by Madhouse Studios, the anime is based on the manga written and illustrated by Naoki Urasawa. The series follows the story of Dr. Kenzo Tenma, a Japanese brain surgeon living in Germany, who becomes embroiled in a dangerous game of cat and mouse with a serial killer known as Johan Liebert. The series explores themes of nihilism, morality, humanity, and the consequences of one’s actions. In this article, we will review the Monster anime and provide an in-depth analysis of its plot, characters, themes, and overall impact.
